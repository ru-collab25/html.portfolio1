About Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani

"Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani" (YJHD) is a 2013 Bollywood romantic drama directed by Ayan Mukerji and produced by Karan Johar. It stars Ranbir Kapoor as Bunny, Deepika Padukone as Naina, Kalki Koechlin as Aditi, and Aditya Roy Kapur as Avi.


Naina Talwar, a shy and studious medical student, decides to take a break from her routine life and joins a trekking trip to Manali. On this trip, she reunites with her former classmates: the adventurous Kabir "Bunny" Thapar, the free-spirited Aditi, and the fun-loving Avi. Over the course of the trek, Naina begins to open up, forming a deep bond with the group, and secretly falls for Bunny. However, Bunny is a wanderer at heart, driven by his dream of traveling the world and uninterested in settling down. During the wedding, Bunny and Naina reconnect, and their feelings for each other resurface. Bunny begins to question his priorities and realizes that love and relationships are as important as his ambitions.
